Why Chinese models are so cheap

The cheapest LLM APIs in 2026 all come from Chinese providers: DeepSeek, StepFun, Alibaba (Qwen), ByteDance (Doubao), MiniMax, and Zhipu (GLM). Here's why they can offer such low prices:

  • Government subsidies: China's AI development is heavily subsidized, reducing infrastructure costs.
  • Massive scale: These providers serve millions of domestic users, achieving economies of scale.
  • Competitive market: Intense domestic competition drives prices down.
  • Efficient architectures: Many use mixture-of-experts (MoE) designs that activate only a fraction of parameters per request.

Importantly, low price doesn't mean low quality. DeepSeek V4 matches or exceeds GPT-5.4 on coding benchmarks while costing 95% less. The performance gap has largely closed.
