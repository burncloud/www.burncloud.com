Frequently Asked Questions

How is billing calculated?+
Billing is per token, split into input and output. You pay the model's input rate for every token you send and the output rate for every token it returns, metered per 1M tokens. No monthly fees, no seat charges.
Do credits expire?+
No. Credits never expire — whatever you load stays on your balance until you spend it.
Can I get a refund?+
Yes. Unused credits are refundable to your original payment method on request.
Is there a free tier?+
Creating an account is free and needs no credit card. You add credits only when you're ready to make calls; the minimum top-up is $5.
What payment methods do you accept?+
All major cards via Stripe. Credits appear on your balance instantly after payment.
