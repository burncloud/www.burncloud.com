Frequently asked questions

What is the minimum spend?+
There's no monthly minimum. Accounts are free to create, and the minimum credit top-up is $5, which never expires.
Can I use a free tier?+
Creating an account is free with no credit card. You only pay for the tokens you actually use once you add credits.
What if I need to scale quickly?+
Rate limits scale with your credit balance, up to thousands of requests per minute on request, so a launch spike is handled without a plan change.
Is there a student discount?+
Because pricing is already at or below official rates with zero markup, there's no separate student tier — everyone pays the same low per-token price.
Can I self-host for free?+
Yes. The gateway is MIT licensed and self-hostable at no cost; you only pay the upstream providers directly.
